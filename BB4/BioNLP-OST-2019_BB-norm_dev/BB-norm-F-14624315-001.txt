A number of transaminases with different substrate specificities 
have been described (Engels et al. 2000; Yvon et al. 1998). These enzymes are reported for several Lactococcus lactis, Lactobacillus, Streptococcus and Propionibacterium species (Engels et al. 2000; Hansen et al. 2001; Thierry et al. 2002).
